Bio

I'm a PhD student at CS department of Rice University advised by Prof. Ang Chen (starting from 2018 fall). I got my BS and MS degrees from Beihang University in 2014 and 2017, respectively. Before joining Rice, I was an OS kernel developer in VMware Inc. from 2017.1 to 2018.6.

My current research focus is networked system security, especially the security implications of programmable data planes.
