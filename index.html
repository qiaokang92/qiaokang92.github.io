About Me

I received my BS and MS degrees from Beihang University in 2014 and 2017, respectively. I was an OS kernel developer in VMware Inc. from 2017.1 to 2018.6. I am a PhD student at CS department of Rice University, advised by Prof. Ang Chen, starting from 2018 Fall.

My research focus at Rice is Programmable In-network Security (Poise), which seeks to make futures networks fundamentally more secure with programmable data planes.

I plan to leave the PhD program at Rice at the end of 2020. I'll be on the industry job market. If you have openings and think we are a good match, please drop me an email at qiaokang1213 at gmail dot com. Thank you!
