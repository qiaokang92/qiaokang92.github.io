About Me

I received my BS and MS degrees from Beihang University in 2014 and 2017, respectively. I was an OS kernel developer in VMware Inc. from 2017.1 to 2018.6. From 2018 to 2020, I pursued the PhD degree at CS department of Rice University, advised by Prof. Ang Chen. My research at Rice focused Programmable In-network Security (Poise), aiming at making futures networks more secure with programmable data planes. I left the PhD program at the end of 2020 and joined industry.
